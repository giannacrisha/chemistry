Types of Bonds

Ionic Bond

Formed by metals and nonmetals
Bond formed by ions
High electronegativity difference

Covalent Bond

Formed by nonmetals
Bond formed by sharing electrons
Low electronegativity difference

Metallic Bond

Formed by metals
Bond formed when positive ions are glued together by electrons
Low electronegativity difference
