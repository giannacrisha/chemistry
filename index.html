Types of Bonds
Ionic Bond
Bond formed by ions where the one with more valence electrons attract the one with less valence electrons
1A pairs with 7A
2A pairs with 6A
3A pairs with 5A

Covalent Bond
Bond formed by sharing electrons
A line indicates two electrons are shared

Metallic Bond
Bond formed when positive ions are glued together by electrons

metals
-conductors
-malleable
-often solid at room temperature

non-metals
-insulator
-non-malleable
-often gas at room temperature

metalloids
-lovechild of metals and nonmetals

prediction of bond
metal and nonmetal -- ionic bond
nonmetal and nonmetal -- covalent bond
metal and metal -- metallic bond
